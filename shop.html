zine + stainless steel cup

FIRST ISSUE / GUERRILLA BUILDS / KEYS

Issue 001 is a key into a matrix of guerrilla furniture on and around the UC Berkeley campus. Places to connect—with place and with people—each with a different goal, under the same loose guidelines: done with purpose, without permission, and with what is available.

Each set includes one printed zine with the locations of all current active builds and one stainless steel cup for the beverage of your choice—from coffee to wine. The zine functions as a map and “key”; the cup acts as an open invitation to future gatherings. Once purchased, you will be added to a mailing list.
